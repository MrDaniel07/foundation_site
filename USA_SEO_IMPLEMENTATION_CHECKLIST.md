## ‚ö†Ô∏è Common Pitfalls to Avoid

1. **Don't change URLs** - Keep all page URLs the same
2. **Don't remove old content** - Maintain 301 redirects if changing anything
3. **Don't keyword stuff** - Use keywords naturally in content
4. **Don't buy backlinks** - Only pursue legitimate, high-quality links
5. **Don't ignore Google Search Console warnings** - Address crawl errors immediately
6. **Don't mix in competitor keywords** - Focus on your own brand and mission
7. **Don't forget to set GSC geo-targeting** - This is critical!
